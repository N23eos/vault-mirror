# 07 — Growth Areas

## Goal

Name the areas where development would give the highest return, prioritized by
impact — grounded in patterns from the vault, not generic self-help.

## Method

- Pull raw material from what earlier modules surfaced (cycles, values gaps,
  thinking blind side) plus fresh scanning: recurring complaints, repeated
  struggles, skills the person keeps wishing for, feedback from others quoted
  in notes.
- For each candidate area: how often does it show up, across how many years,
  what does it demonstrably cost, and is there evidence of past attempts to
  address it (and why those attempts stalled)?
- Prioritize by: recurrence × cost × leverage (would fixing this unlock other
  areas?). Cap the list — 3 to 5 areas. A list of ten is a list of zero.

## Output contract — `Analysis/07-growth-areas.md`

```
# Growth Areas
## Priority list       (3–5, ordered, one line each)
## Area deep-dives     (per area: pattern, evidence across time, cost,
                        past attempts and why they stalled, leverage)
## Explicitly deprioritized  (candidates cut, and why)
```

## Rules reminder

Apply the global rules from `prompts/00-start.md`.

## Quality bar

- Is each area backed by a multi-year pattern, not a single bad week?
- Does each deep-dive include past attempts? (If none found, say so.)
- Would the person recognize themselves, or is this generic advice?
