# 08 — Open Questions

## Goal

Surface the questions the material itself poses — the unresolved tensions,
recurring dilemmas, and decisions the person keeps circling without landing.
This module asks; it does not answer.

## Method

- Look for explicit questions the person asks themselves repeatedly across
  notes and years, especially those that reappear rephrased.
- Look for implicit questions: contradictions between notes, decisions
  perpetually postponed, "someday" items that never move, topics approached
  and backed away from.
- For each question, trace its history: when it first appears, how it evolves,
  what answers were tried and rejected.
- Rank by weight: which questions, if answered, would change the most?

## Output contract — `Analysis/08-open-questions.md`

```
# Open Questions
## The big questions   (3–7, each: the question, its history in the vault,
                        answers tried, why it's still open)
## Quiet questions     (implicit tensions the person may not see as questions)
## What answering would change  (per big question, one paragraph)
```

## Rules reminder

Apply the global rules from `prompts/00-start.md`.

## Quality bar

- Is every question traceable to actual notes, not projected onto the person?
- Did you resist answering? Formulating well is the whole job here.
