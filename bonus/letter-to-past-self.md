# Bonus — Letter to Past Self

## Goal

Write a letter from the person today to themselves at a specific past point —
using only what the vault proves they have learned since. Not fiction: a
compression of real, evidenced growth.

**Requires:** modules 01, 03 complete (chronicle provides the timeline).

## Method

- Pick the addressee moment with the user, or default to the biggest turning
  point found in `Analysis/03-chronicle.md`.
- Collect what the person at that moment believed, feared, and planned — from
  notes of that period only.
- Collect what actually happened and what was learned — from later notes.
- The letter may only contain knowledge the vault shows was actually gained.
  No invented wisdom, no "everything works out" unless it evidently did.
- Write in the person's own voice (sample their phrasing), warm but honest.

## Output contract — `Analysis/letter-to-past-self.md`

```
# Letter to Past Self
## To: (the moment, and why this moment)
## The letter          (1–2 pages)
## Appendix: receipts  (each claim in the letter → the note that proves it)
```

## Rules reminder

Apply the global rules from `prompts/00-start.md`.

## Quality bar

- Could every sentence of advice be footnoted to a real later note?
- Does it sound like them, not like a greeting card?
