# 09 — Executive Summary

## Goal

Synthesize modules 01–08 into one honest portrait a stranger could read in ten
minutes and understand who this person is, where they are, and what the
central dynamic of their life currently is.

**Run this only after 01–08 are complete.** Check `Analysis/_progress.md`.

## Method

- Re-read all files in `Analysis/` (01–08). Do not re-scan the vault except to
  verify a claim you are about to promote to the summary.
- Find the through-line: the one dynamic that connects the thinking style, the
  cycles, the values gaps, and the open questions. Most lives have one or two
  central tensions — name them.
- Promote only conclusions marked `confirmed` or `likely` into hard claims;
  hypotheses stay clearly labeled or get cut.
- Resolve contradictions between modules explicitly rather than averaging
  them away.

## Output contract — `Analysis/09-executive-summary.md`

```
# Executive Summary
## Portrait in one page   (who this person is, evidenced)
## The central dynamic    (the tension/engine that explains the most)
## Current position       (where they are right now vs their trajectory)
## Strongest assets       (top 3, from module 06)
## Highest-leverage areas (top 3, from module 07)
## The one question       (from module 08 — the question that matters most)
## Contradictions between modules  (and how you resolved them)
```

## Rules reminder

Apply the global rules from `prompts/00-start.md`.

## Quality bar

- Can every sentence in the portrait be traced back to a module (which can be
  traced to notes)?
- Is the central dynamic specific to this person, or would it fit anyone?
- Ten-minute read. Cut until it hurts.
