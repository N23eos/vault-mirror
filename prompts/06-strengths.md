# 06 — Strengths

## Goal

Identify real, evidenced strengths — including ones the person underuses or
doesn't recognize as strengths. This is the one module that looks for assets,
and it must be exactly as rigorous as the critical ones.

## Method

- Hunt for demonstrated capability, not self-praise: problems actually solved,
  things built or finished, moments where the notes show unusual skill
  (analysis, writing, persistence, connection-making, honesty with self).
- Look for strengths visible in the vault's form itself: consistency of
  journaling, quality of thinking under stress, ability to articulate.
- Specifically hunt underused strengths: capabilities that show up clearly
  but rarely, or only in low-stakes contexts, or that the person dismisses in
  their own words.
- Discard anything that is flattery without a specific behavioral trace.

## Output contract — `Analysis/06-strengths.md`

```
# Strengths
## Core strengths       (3–6, each: what it is, evidence, where it shows)
## Underused strengths  (present but not deployed — evidence of both)
## Strengths under stress  (what holds up when things get hard)
## Anti-inflation check (what was considered and rejected for lack of evidence)
```

## Rules reminder

Apply the global rules from `prompts/00-start.md`.

## Quality bar

- Would each strength survive a skeptic asking "show me where"?
- Does the anti-inflation section exist and contain real rejections?
- No strength stated in a way the person could get for free from a horoscope.
